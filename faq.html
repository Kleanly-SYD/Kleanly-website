End of tenancy

Our end of tenancy clean covers every room thoroughly — oven cleaning (including trays), internal windows and sills, cupboard and drawer interiors, light fittings, skirting boards, range hoods, and extractor fans. See our full checklist for the complete room-by-room breakdown. Optional add-ons (carpet cleaning, balcony, garage, etc.) can be added at booking.
No — carpet cleaning is an optional add-on. We recommend it if you have pets, children, have lived in the property for several years, or if your agent has specifically requested it. Staircases and hallways each count as a separate area.
Not reliably. Residential carpet cleaning is designed to improve hygiene — extracting dust, allergens, and general soiling — not to remove set-in stains. Light or superficial marks may improve, but stain removal is not guaranteed. For heavily stained carpets, an industrial specialist may be needed.
An area is any carpeted space up to 10 square metres — a bedroom, dining room, lounge, hallway, staircase, foyer, or walk-in wardrobe. Larger open-plan spaces count as multiple areas. For example, a 13m² living/dining area counts as 2 areas.
Yes — in the vast majority of cases. Our quotes are fixed based on the information you provide. They assume: accurate bedroom/bathroom counts, all relevant add-ons selected, a property in typical move-out condition, and no unusual access restrictions. If our team arrives and finds the property significantly larger or in a much worse condition than described, we'll contact you before proceeding with any additional time.
If your agent or property manager raises any cleaning concerns after our visit, we'll return within 72 hours and re-clean those areas at no charge. The guarantee applies to the checklist items included in your booking — optional add-ons that weren't selected are not covered.

What we don't clean

  • Chandeliers or individual light bulbs
  • Fireplaces or flues
  • Fly screens
  • Ceiling fans (blades only, not motors)
  • Air conditioning units or filters
  • Picture rails or cornices above reach
  • Biohazards or hazardous waste
  • Silicone remediation or grout re-sealing
  • Guaranteed stain removal from carpets
  • Embedded pet hair in carpet
  • Furniture removal or cleaning underneath
  • Odour removal treatments
  • Paint removal or touch-ups
  • Exterior walls or roofs
